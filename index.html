Welcome to WJTB Radio!

We're an undergraduate student organization at NJIT focused on broadcasting radio shows, handling sound at events, and providing audio recording services for our members!

Our broadcast lineup currently features over 15 shows hosted by students, airing throughout the week. These shows span a wide variety of music from indie, electronic, classic rock, and will even host talk shows and dramatic readings. Make sure you check out our schedule so you don't miss out!
